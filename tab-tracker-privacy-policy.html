Privacy Policy for Tab Tracker

Last Updated: 2025 4th January

Tab Tracker ("the Extension") is a browser extension designed to provide users with an easy way to manage their open tabs. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights concerning your information.

1. Information We Collect

The Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • Tab Data: The URLs of open tabs are only accessed locally within your browser for the purpose of displaying and copying them. This data is not sent to any server or third party.
  • No Tracking: The Extension does not track or monitor your browsing activity.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data accessed by the Extension is used exclusively to:

  • Display the list of open tabs in the current browser window.
  • Allow you to copy URLs for personal use.

All data is processed locally on your device and is not shared or stored.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent any data collected or accessed by the Extension. Your information remains private and is never transmitted to external servers.

4. Permissions

The Extension requires the following browser permissions:

  • Access to Tabs: This permission is used to read the titles and URLs of tabs open in the current window. This data is processed locally and is never stored or transmitted.

5. Security

We take appropriate measures to ensure that the Extension operates securely and respects your privacy. The Extension's code does not include any functionality to store, transmit, or misuse your data.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the Extension's functionality or legal requirements. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.
